# Solution #3

## Position Sizing

This is professional risk management.

### Rule

Never risk more than 1% per trade.

Example:

Capital:

```text
₹2,00,000
```

1% Risk:

```text
₹2,000
```

Maximum loss:

```text
₹2,000
```

No matter what.

---

### Formula

```text
Position Size =
Maximum Risk
÷
Stop Loss Distance
```

Example:

```text
Risk = ₹2000

Stop Loss = ₹50
```

Shares:

```text
2000 ÷ 50

= 40 shares
```
